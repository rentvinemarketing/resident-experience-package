the four pillars

Everything a resident needs.
Already in Rentvine.

HOME isn't a checklist. It's a commitment to every resident, at every stage of their journey.

Pillar 01
Hospitality
"You shouldn't have to figure it out alone."

A warm, guided move-in experience from the moment a lease is signed. Residents know exactly what to do and what to expect, without calling anyone.

  • Guided move-in checklist, day one
  • Welcome message from their property manager
  • Lease, documents, and contacts, all in one place
  • Move-in photo documentation
Pillar 02
Ongoing Care
"We don't disappear after move-in day."

Real support that residents can feel from the first work order to the last check-in. No calls. No chasing. Just fixed.

  • Work order submission with photos or video
  • Real-time status updates
  • Proactive check-ins from the property manager
  • Consistent communication throughout the lease
Pillar 03
Member Benefits
"You're not just a tenant. You're a member."

Perks, resources, and services that make a rental feel like it's working for the resident, not against them. Already here because they're part of HOME.

  • Renter's insurance, pre-filled and ready
  • Utility concierge and move-in discounts
  • Local resources and community perks
  • Resident rewards program
Pillar 04
Experience
"It all adds up to something that feels like home."

Hospitality, care, and benefits don't mean anything without the feeling they create. Experience is what residents remember and why they renew.

  • A consistent experience from lease signing to move-out
  • Residents who feel seen, supported, and valued
  • Higher renewal rates, fewer complaints
  • A package PMs can pitch with confidence
