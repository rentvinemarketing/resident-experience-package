What is a Resident Experience Package?

A Resident Experience Package (also known as a Resident Benefits Package or RXP) is a collection of services, benefits, and perks designed to enhance the rental experience. Offered by property management companies as part of the lease, these packages typically include valuable resident services for a small monthly fee, making everyday renting more convenient, rewarding, and worry-free.

what is HOME

Built in.
Not bolted on.

Other resident benefit packages are third-party add-ons: separate logins, disconnected data, and another vendor to manage. HOME is built directly into Rentvine's software, and built around each resident. Turn features on or off by property, lease, or resident preference. No gaps. No middlemen. No one-size-fits-all.
